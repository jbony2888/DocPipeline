Essay: My dad has always been there for me, even though he doesn't live with me any more. He's taught me so much, and has made sure to always take care of me. People don't understand him. He seems weak and old, but we see him as smart and collected. My dad made sure to teach me that a man doesn't mean to have big muscles, talk loudly, be impulsive or even to be rich. He has taught me that a man means to be calm, collected and to put others before myself. I have never really had any frustrations with my father, he's always been very understanding, and never makes me feel less about anything. One of my favorite memories that I have with my dad is going out for ice cream with him, during my sister's dance practice. There was time I had to go with him and we hadn't eaten, so we would go to this local taco shop and he would get me a bag of little chips that I really liked. My dad has shown me that a man can show his emotion, its one of the best things he's shown me because in doing so it allowed me to express myself. This was very important to me because it has allowed me to express my emotions, and has enabled me to get a lot of weight off my shoulders.
