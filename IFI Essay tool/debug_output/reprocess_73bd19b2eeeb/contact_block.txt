Essay: My Father 
 
 A good father figure is always patient. They shouldn't be prone to anger or quick to yell. A good 
father figure is someone who is kind. Someone who picks you up after you fall and kisses an 
injured knee. Lastly, a good father figure should try to be there for you, even if sometimes they 
can't make it somewhere or they're five minutes late, they should at least try. 
 
 My father isn't perfect, but he tries. There have been many times where I've been stubborn or 
been frustrated with him for a small thing, but he's patient. I can get annoyed quickly and I 
understand that I'm not the easiest to deal with most times but I appreciate my dad for staying 
patient with me. My dad stays kind to me. He can recognize when he's done something wrong 
or made me upset and he can apologize for it without making a ton of excuses for himself. My 
dad is busy, he has to work hard to provide for me and he's tired almost everyday. But my dad
