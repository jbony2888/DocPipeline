When people hear the word father/dad, they might think it only means a man who has children. 
Except, fatherhood is much deeper than that. Being a father is not only just about giving a child food, 
clothes, and a place to live. It is about being present, teaching important lessons, and showing love 
every single day. A father plays a huge role in helping a child grow into a responsible and confident 
adult.
