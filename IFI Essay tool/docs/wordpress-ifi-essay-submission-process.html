New Essay Submission Process

We’ve simplified how essay entries are submitted and organized so teachers can spend less time on data entry and more time supporting students.

Important: sign in with your email first. The submission tool does not show a file upload box until you are logged in. After you open the tool, you will see a page asking for your email address—that is the correct first step.
  1. Click Open Essay Submission Tool below.
  2. Enter the email address you want to use (often your CPS or school email). Click to send yourself a secure login link.
  3. Check your inbox and spam/junk folder for an email with a login link. Click the link within about one hour (links expire).
  4. After the link opens, you should land in the tool with the upload / drag-and-drop area visible. Then you can add PDFs or images.
If the login link shows an error: copy the entire link from the email, paste it into Chrome or Safari, and press Enter. Microsoft Outlook sometimes alters links—try opening the message in Outlook on the web or request a new link from the login page. Use the same browser to open the email link that you used to request the link when possible.

Submit Essays in One Batch

Information Is Extracted Automatically

The system reads each form and pulls key details like student name, school, and grade. Any missing information is clearly flagged for review.

Fix and Approve in Bulk

Teachers can quickly apply the same school or grade to multiple entries at once, then approve completed records with a single click.

4

Automatically Organized for Review

Approved entries are organized by school and grade, making them easy to review and export.
